🏷️ Kategori LayananService Categories

Kategori adalah fondasi portal layanan. Pengguna memilih kategori saat mengajukan tiket, dan kategori menentukan bagaimana tiket diproses, prefix nomor tiket, dan tampilannya di portal.

Categories are the foundation of the service portal. Users select a category when submitting a ticket, and the category determines how tickets are processed, the ticket number prefix, and how they appear on the portal.

When a SupportDesk staff submits an edit request for a Closed ticket, SuperAdmin (and Manager) receives a notification to review and decide.

1

4
Atau klik Tolak → isi alasan penolakan (dianjurkan) → konfirmasiOr click Reject → fill in rejection reason (recommended) → confirm
